Feature 1

Steel bars are a type of construction material used to build structures such as buildings, bridges, dams, RCC roads, and towers. It can be used in concrete to continue providing tensile strength because the tensile strength of concrete is significantly lower than the compressive strength.

Feature 2

Steel coil is made from a finished steel product such as sheet or strip that has been wound or coiled after rolling; its width is much greater than the thickness. Depending on the production method, steel coil can be classified as Hot Rolled, Cold Rolled, or Galvanized.
